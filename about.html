Our Legacy

Nurturing Nature with Scientific Precision

Founded in 2011, ArborPro began with a simple mission: to bridge the gap between traditional tree care and modern forest biology.

What started as a small team of two passionate climbers has grown into a leading force in urban forestry throughout the region. We don't just "cut trees"; we manage living assets that provide oxygen, shade, and historical value to our communities.

The Roots of Our Success

Our values define every cut we make and every tree we plant. We are committed to the long-term health of our environment.

Tree Health First

We prioritize preservation over removal. Our goal is to extend the life of every tree through scientific health assessments and proactive care.

Safety Standard

Tree work is dangerous. We maintain a zero-incident culture through continuous training and utilizing state-of-the-art rigging technology.

Eco-Stewardship

We repurposed 100% of our wood waste into organic mulch or lumber, ensuring that nothing from nature goes to waste.
