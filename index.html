Ask a Different Question
When is the best time to prune my trees?
While most trees can be pruned year-round, the dormant season (late winter) is generally best for the health of the tree as it minimizes stress and reduces the risk of pest infestation.
Are you fully insured for hazardous removals?
Yes, ArborPro carries $5M in general liability insurance and comprehensive worker's compensation. We provide certificates of insurance upon request for every project.
How do I know if my tree is diseased?
Look for signs like leaf discoloration, premature leaf drop, fungus growth on the bark, or dead branches. Our certified arborists can provide a professional diagnostic assessment.
Do you offer stump removal with tree removal?
Stump grinding is a separate service, but we often bundle it with tree removal. We can grind stumps to 6-12 inches below grade, allowing you to replant or turf the area.
